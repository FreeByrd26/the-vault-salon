Our Stylists

Owner & Operator — IBE® Elite Artist

Nicole Strachan

Nicole, a seasoned professional with over two decades of experience in the beauty industry, brings her expertise from some of the most prestigious hair salons in Metro Detroit. Her enthusiasm for enhancing people’s beauty is evident from the first interaction.

With a deep understanding of the latest hair trends and top-tier products, Nicole ensures her clients not only look their best but also feel extraordinary. As a certified hair extension specialist and a master colorist, she excels in providing personalized hair solutions.

Nicole specializes in Invisible Bead Extensions™ — former Platform Artist for 2 years, now Elite Artist — and is also skilled in Great Lengths™ Extensions, offering a comprehensive and exceptional beauty experience.

Stylist

Lisa Becker

Lisa has been a colorist/stylist for fourteen years. She has trained on both coasts and worked in well known salons in Connecticut as well as Los Angeles. Now proudly joining the Vault team.

Lisa specializes in customizing a look that enhances each individual. She pays detailed attention & consideration to skin tone, bone structure, eye color, lifestyle, & personal style, keeping the client’s goals in mind.

Stylist — Extensions & Luxury Styling

Olivia Martin

Olivia Martin grew up in Kansas City, Missouri, before spending four years in sunny Sarasota, Florida, where she discovered her passion for hairstyling. Since becoming a licensed stylist, she’s been dedicated to mastering her craft, earning her certification in extensions.

Now part of The Vault Team in New Canaan, Olivia specializes in creating flawless, natural-looking extensions and color. She works Tuesday through Saturday, focusing exclusively on extensions and luxury styling. She’s passionate about helping her clients feel confident and stunning every day.
